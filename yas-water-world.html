About This Experience

Arba Tourism offers an exciting opportunity for thrill-seekers to experience Yas Waterworld in Abu Dhabi. This water park is a hub of adventure and fun, featuring over 40 rides, slides, and attractions.

Visitors can experience the region's unique culture with attractions such as the Pearl Diving Experience and the Al Raha River. The park also features restaurants serving delicious food.

Whether you're looking for an exciting day out with friends or a fun-filled family adventure, Yas Waterworld is the perfect destination.
