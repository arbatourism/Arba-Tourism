Yas Water World

Arba Tourism's

Yas Water World

Arba Tourism offers an exciting opportunity for thrill-seekers to experience Yas Waterworld in Abu Dhabi. This water park is a hub of adventure and fun, featuring over 40 rides, slides, and attractions. It has something for everyone, from adrenaline-pumping rollercoasters and extreme water slides to calm, relaxing pools for families and young children.

Visitors to Yas Waterworld can experience the region's unique culture and heritage with attractions such as the Pearl Diving Experience and the Al Raha River. The park also features a range of restaurants and cafes, serving up delicious food and beverages for visitors to enjoy between adventures.

Whether you're looking for an exciting day out with friends or a fun-filled family adventure, Yas Waterworld is the perfect destination for those seeking an unforgettable experience.
