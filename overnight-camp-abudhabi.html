Overnight Camp Safari In Abu Dhabi

Experience the ultimate desert adventure with our overnight camp safari in Abu Dhabi. Our expert guides will take you on a thrilling ride through the rolling dunes, providing breathtaking views of the desert as the sun sets on the horizon.

After an exciting evening of dune bashing and desert activities like sandboarding, camel riding and falcon shows, settle in for the night in our comfortable campsite. Our tents are equipped with all the necessary amenities, including comfortable bedding, hot showers, and toilet facilities.

As the night falls, enjoy a traditional BBQ dinner with local specialties, followed by traditional entertainment like belly dancing and tanoura dance. After dinner, gaze up at the starry sky as our expert guides take you on a memorable stargazing experience, pointing out constellations and sharing fascinating insights about the night sky.

Wake up to a beautiful desert sunrise and a hot cup of Arabic coffee or tea. Our overnight camp safari also includes a delicious breakfast, providing the perfect start to another day of desert adventure.

At Arba Tourism, safety is our top priority. Our experienced drivers and guides ensure that every journey is smooth and secure, while our top-of-the-line vehicles are equipped with all the necessary safety features. Embark on an unforgettable journey that captures the mystical charm of the Arabian desert with our overnight camp safari.
