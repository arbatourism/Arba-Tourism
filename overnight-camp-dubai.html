About This Tour

Welcome to our overnight camp safari in Dubai, where we offer an unforgettable experience that captures the essence of Arabian hospitality amidst the breathtaking beauty of the desert.

Our experienced and certified drivers will take you on an exciting journey through the desert, where you'll indulge in thrilling activities such as dune bashing, sandboarding, camel riding and falconry. As the sun sets, enjoy traditional Arabic cuisine under the starry night sky.
