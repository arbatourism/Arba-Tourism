Overnight Camp Safari In Dubai

Welcome to our overnight camp safari in Dubai, where we offer an unforgettable experience that captures the essence of Arabian hospitality amidst the breathtaking beauty of the desert. Our campsite is located in the heart of the Dubai desert, where you'll be surrounded by the stunning sand dunes and the magical tranquility of the desert landscape.

Our experienced and certified drivers will take you on an exciting journey through the desert, where you'll have the opportunity to indulge in a variety of thrilling activities such as dune bashing, sandboarding, camel riding and falconry. Our guides are knowledgeable and passionate about the desert, and they'll ensure that you have a safe and enjoyable experience.

As the sun sets over the desert, we'll set up a luxurious campsite where you can relax and unwind. You'll be welcomed with traditional Arabic coffee and dates, and we'll serve you a delicious dinner of authentic Arabic cuisine, while you enjoy the warmth of the bonfire and the beauty of the starry night sky.

After a night under the stars, wake up to the stunning sunrise and start your day with a delicious breakfast before heading back to the city. Our overnight camp safari is an experience like no other, offering a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the beauty and culture of the Arabian desert. Join us for an unforgettable adventure that you'll remember for a lifetime.
