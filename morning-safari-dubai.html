Morning Safari in Dubai

Get ready for an adventure-packed day in the heart of the Arabian desert with our Morning Desert Safari in Dubai. Our experienced guides will take you on a thrilling journey through the rolling dunes, as you immerse yourself in the timeless charm of the desert.

Our fleet of top-of-the-line vehicles will take you on an exhilarating ride over the dunes, allowing you to take in the breathtaking views of the desert landscape. Get your adrenaline pumping with our exciting activities such as sandboarding and dune bashing, as you surf down the steep dunes on a specially designed board.

For a more traditional experience, hop on the back of a gentle camel and enjoy a relaxing ride through the dunes. Our expert guides will be on hand to provide fascinating insights into the local culture and traditions.

After an action-packed morning of adventure, it's time to unwind with a delicious lunch spread featuring local delicacies and refreshing beverages. Take a break in the shade of a traditional Bedouin tent, and soak in the tranquil desert atmosphere.

With our Morning Desert Safari in Dubai, you'll have a one-of-a-kind experience that showcases the natural beauty and cultural richness of the Arabian desert.
