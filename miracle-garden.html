WORLD'S LARGEST NATURAL FLOWER GARDEN

About This Experience

If you're looking to experience a truly breathtaking display of nature's beauty, the Miracle Garden Dubai is an attraction that should be at the top of your list. Spanning over 72,000 square meters, this stunning garden is home to more than 45 million blooming flowers and plants in vibrant colors.

Visitors can stroll through multiple themed areas, each offering a different experience. From the heart-shaped pathway adorned with thousands of petunias to the giant floral clock that blooms with different flowers every season, there is something to captivate everyone's attention.
