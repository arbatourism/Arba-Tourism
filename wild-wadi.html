Wild Wadi in Dubai

Wild Wadi is a waterpark located in Dubai that provides visitors with an unforgettable experience of thrilling water rides and attractions. With more than 30 rides and attractions, Wild Wadi offers something for everyone, from families with young children to thrill-seekers looking for an adrenaline rush. One of the most popular attractions is the Jumeirah Sceirah, a high-speed water slide that reaches speeds of up to 80 km/h, and propels riders through a tunnel that passes through a shark-filled lagoon. For those looking for a more relaxed experience, the park also features a lazy river and a wave pool.

Arba Tourism offers visitors to Dubai the opportunity to experience Wild Wadi as part of their package tours.
