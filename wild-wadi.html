About This Experience

Wild Wadi is a waterpark located in Dubai that provides visitors with an unforgettable experience of thrilling water rides and attractions. With more than 30 rides and attractions, Wild Wadi offers something for everyone, from families with young children to thrill-seekers looking for an adrenaline rush.

Arba Tourism offers visitors to Dubai the opportunity to experience Wild Wadi as part of their package tours. With our expert guidance and transport services, visitors can enjoy a hassle-free trip to the park and spend a fun-filled day with family and friends.
