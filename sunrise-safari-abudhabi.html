Sunrise Desert Safari in Abu Dhabi

Experience the magical beauty of Abu Dhabi's desert landscape with our sunrise desert safari. As the sun rises over the horizon, the landscape is transformed into a stunning array of colors and shadows, providing a breathtaking backdrop for a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

In addition to witnessing the stunning sunrise, our sunrise desert safari also includes a range of exciting activities, including:

  • Dune bashing: Our skilled drivers will take you on a thrilling ride through the rolling dunes, showcasing the power and agility of our top-of-the-line vehicles.
  • Camel rides: Experience the traditional way of transportation in the desert, as you ride through the dunes on the back of a gentle camel.
  • Sandboarding: Try your hand at surfing down the steep dunes on a specially-designed board, providing an exhilarating and unique way to experience the desert landscape.
  • Traditional breakfast: Enjoy a delicious breakfast of local specialties as you watch the sunrise, surrounded by the peaceful serenity of the desert.
