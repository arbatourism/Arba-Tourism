Dubai Marina
Dhow Cruise Dinner

Dubai's Floating Paradise

Experience the vibrant energy of Dubai Marina from the water. A 90-minute journey through luxury, culture, and unforgettable moments.

About This Experience

Step aboard a beautifully restored wooden dhow for an enchanting evening along the shimmering waters of Dubai Marina. Modeled after a Venetian-style canal, this 90-minute cruise offers front-row views of Dubai's architectural marvels — from luxurious yachts and high-rise towers to the stunning waterfront skyline.

Enjoy a welcome drink upon boarding, then settle into either the air-conditioned lower deck or the open-air upper deck. Savor an international 4-star buffet dinner featuring both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, paired with unlimited refreshments.

As the dhow glides through the Marina, enjoy mesmerizing Tanura dance performances and soft background music, adding a touch of culture and vibrance to your evening. Perfect for couples, families, or business guests, this experience combines fine dining with unforgettable views of the city by night.

Capital's Waterfront Elegance

Discover the elegance of Abu Dhabi from the Corniche waters. A 2-hour journey combining heritage, luxury, and spectacular views.

About This Experience

Step back in time aboard a traditional Arabian dhow and experience Abu Dhabi's skyline like never before. This two-hour evening cruise along the Corniche waters offers an elegant dining experience infused with Emirati heritage and world-class hospitality.

Upon boarding, our friendly crew will escort you to your table as you settle into a warm, inviting ambiance. As the dhow sets sail, enjoy a lavish international buffet featuring an array of appetizers, main courses, and desserts — blending Arabian flavors with global cuisine.

Choose between the fully air-conditioned lower deck or the partly open upper deck, both offering exceptional comfort and uninterrupted views of the illuminated city skyline. With a relaxed atmosphere, delicious food, and spectacular scenery, it's the perfect choice for couples, families, or corporate guests.
