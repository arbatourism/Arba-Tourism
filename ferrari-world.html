Ferrari World

Ferrari World is a thrilling theme park that offers an unforgettable experience for both car enthusiasts and thrill-seekers. As one of the most popular attractions in Abu Dhabi, Arba Tourism offers its customers the chance to visit and explore the excitement of Ferrari World. With a range of exhilarating rides and attractions, Ferrari World has something for everyone. From the world's fastest roller coaster, Formula Rossa, to a 4D racing simulation that puts you in the driver's seat of a Ferrari, visitors can experience the rush of speed and excitement in a safe and controlled environment. In addition to the thrilling rides, Ferrari World also offers a range of shows, exhibits, and dining options, including authentic Italian cuisine at the park's many restaurants. With Arba Tourism's expert guides and convenient transportation, customers can relax and enjoy all that Ferrari World has to offer without the hassle of planning or navigating on their own.
