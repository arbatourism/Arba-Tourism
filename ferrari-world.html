About This Experience

Ferrari World is a thrilling theme park that offers an unforgettable experience for both car enthusiasts and thrill-seekers. As one of the most popular attractions in Abu Dhabi, Arba Tourism offers its customers the chance to visit and explore the excitement of Ferrari World.

With a range of exhilarating rides and attractions, Ferrari World has something for everyone. From the world's fastest roller coaster, Formula Rossa, to a 4D racing simulation that puts you in the driver's seat of a Ferrari.

In addition to thrilling rides, Ferrari World also offers shows, exhibits, and authentic Italian cuisine at the park's many restaurants.
