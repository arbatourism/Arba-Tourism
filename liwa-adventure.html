Liwa Desert Tour

Arba Tourism's

Liwa Desert Tour

Arba Tourism offers a one-of-a-kind experience to explore the breathtaking Liwa Desert. Located in the southern region of Abu Dhabi, the Liwa Desert boasts of towering sand dunes and mesmerizing landscapes. This full-day trip offers a unique opportunity to experience the culture and traditions of the region, while also enjoying the thrill of dune bashing and camel riding. The trip includes a visit to the Liwa Oasis, known for its lush date plantations and picturesque scenery.Visitors can also witness traditional Bedouin life and enjoy a sumptuous meal under the stars.

The Liwa Desert trip is a perfect getaway for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse themselves in the tranquility and beauty of the desert. Arba Tourism ensures a comfortable and memorable experience for all its guests.

Mesmerizing Empty Quarter

Liwa Desert, also known as the Empty Quarter, is a mesmerizing and vast desert located in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. The Empty Quarter is renowned for its infinite dunes that stretch as far as the eye can see, creating a captivating landscape that captivates adventurers and photographers alike. Visiting Liwa Desert is a remarkable experience for travelers and photographers who seek to immerse themselves in its vastness.

As part of the Empty Quarter, which is one of the largest deserts in the world, Liwa offers a breathtaking canvas for capturing stunning photographs and creating lasting memories. Surprisingly, within this arid landscape, lies the Liwa Oasis, a region historically cultivated by bedouin tribes. The presence of lush palm forests amidst the desert's expanse adds an unexpected element of beauty and intrigue to the Liwa Desert experience.
