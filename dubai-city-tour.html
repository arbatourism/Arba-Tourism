Dubai City Tour

Embark on a journey of exploration and discovery with Arba Tourism's Dubai City Tour. As one of the world's most vibrant cities, Dubai boasts a rich blend of cultural and modern attractions, and our expert guides will ensure you get to experience the best of both worlds. The tour will take you to the iconic Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest building, where you can marvel at the stunning views of the city from the observation deck. You'll also get to visit the historic Dubai Creek, where you can experience the city's rich cultural heritage by taking an abra ride across the water.

The tour will then take you to the glittering Dubai Mall, where you can shop to your heart's content and witness the beauty of the Dubai Fountain. You'll also get to explore the charming neighborhoods of Jumeirah and Palm Jumeirah, home to some of the city's most luxurious properties. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, our Dubai City Tour is a must-do experience that will leave you with unforgettable memories.
