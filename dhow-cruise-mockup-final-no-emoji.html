Dubai Marina

About This Experience

Step aboard a beautifully restored wooden dhow for an enchanting evening along the shimmering waters of Dubai Marina. Modeled after a Venetian-style canal, this 90-minute cruise offers front-row views of Dubai's architectural marvels — from luxurious yachts and high-rise towers to the stunning waterfront skyline.

Enjoy a welcome drink upon boarding, then settle into either the air-conditioned lower deck or the open-air upper deck. Savor an international 4-star buffet dinner featuring both vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes, paired with unlimited refreshments.

As the dhow glides through the Marina, enjoy mesmerizing Tanura dance performances and soft background music, adding a touch of culture and vibrance to your evening. Perfect for couples, families, or business guests.

Dhow Cruise Dinner
Abu Dhabi Corniche

About This Experience

Discover Abu Dhabi's majestic skyline from the tranquil waters of the Corniche. This 2-hour dhow cruise dinner combines traditional Arabian hospitality with modern comfort, offering you a unique vantage point of the UAE's vibrant capital city.

Glide past the iconic Emirates Palace, the gleaming towers of the Corniche, and Abu Dhabi's impressive waterfront developments. The cruise features an open-air upper deck and a climate-controlled lower deck, both offering spectacular panoramic views.

Indulge in a lavish international buffet featuring Arabic and continental cuisines, complemented by unlimited soft beverages. Live entertainment and traditional performances create an atmosphere of celebration, making this cruise perfect for romantic evenings, family gatherings, or special occasions.
