Tour Details

The Experience

Explore the Arabian Desert like you were in Laurence of Arabia or the Mad Max movies! From your hotel, you'll be picked up and brought to the safari stop where a professional, licensed safari guide will meet you. As it's early in the morning, you'll avoid the fearsome desert heat and enjoy breathtaking desert views comfortably. You'll head up the highest sand dunes and go roaring down the slopes as you go dune bashing.

Pick Up & Duration

Pick up starts around 7:30 AM with comfortable 4x4 vehicles perfect for navigating the desert terrain. The safari includes approximately 4 hours of adventure, with drop-off around 11:30 AM. A convenient hotel pick up and drop off will take you to your desert safari adventure and back!

Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the morning desert safari in Abu Dhabi start and end? ▼

The safari starts with a pickup at 7:30 AM and ends with a drop-off around 11:30 AM.

What activities are included in the morning desert safari? ▼

The safari includes dune bashing, a photo stop in the middle of the desert, sandboarding, and camel riding.

What type of vehicle is used for the safari? ▼

The safari uses comfortable and sturdy 4x4 vehicles (Land Cruisers), perfect for navigating the desert terrain.

Can children participate in the morning desert safari? ▼

Yes, children can participate. There is no age limit for a morning desert safari Abu Dhabi. However, it's recommended to check with us regarding safety guidelines for very young children.

Are there any health restrictions for the safari? ▼

Yes, the safari may not be suitable for pregnant women, people with back problems, or those with serious medical conditions. Please contact us for specific guidelines.
