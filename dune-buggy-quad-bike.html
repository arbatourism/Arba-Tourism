Important Safety Notice

  • Quad biking and buggy driving are self-driving activities taken at your own risk. The activity provider's insurance does not cover accidents or damages.
  • All participants must sign an indemnity form before the activity.
  • Children aged 11 and under will be passengers only on Quad Bikes.
  • Children aged 15 and under will be passengers only on Dune Buggies.
  • Basic driving knowledge is required.
  • International travel insurance is recommended.
  • Entertainment and alcohol are not available during Ramadan.
