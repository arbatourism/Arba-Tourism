VIP Events

At Arba Tourism, we understand that our clients have unique requirements and preferences when it comes to their travel experiences. This is why we offer our VIP Event service, where we can customize your event to your exact specifications. Whether you are planning a group trip, a romantic dinner in the desert, or a corporate event, our experienced team will work with you to create an unforgettable experience. Our VIP Event service includes the option for customizing the lighting and set up as you're your theme, private safari sections and much more... where you can enjoy the stunning natural beauty of the Arabian interruptions.

You can choose from a variety of activities, including team building initiative, group challenges, dune bashing, DJ set up, quad biking, and many more. We can also arrange for special setups like comfortable seating arrangements and private dining areas, so you and your guests can relax and enjoy the scenery while indulging in a sumptuous meal.
